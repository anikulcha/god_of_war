When is the next God of War coming out?

God of War Ragnarök will release on November 9, 2022. To get the latest updates on release timing, make sure you follow Santa Monica Studio on Twitter, Instagram and Facebook!

Will God of War Ragnarök have an upgrade path?

Yes, God of War Ragnarök will offer a digital upgrade* option from a disc or digital PS4 game to the digital PS5 version for an additional cost. To find out more on upgrading PS4 games to PS5, visit our support page. *Upgrade to the digital PS5™ version of the game at a discounted price. To upgrade eligible PS4™ disc copies, players need a PS5™ console with a disc drive. Visit PlayStation.com/help for more information.
